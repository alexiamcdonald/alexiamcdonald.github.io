This is where you can easily find The Melbourne Emoji Game which was originally posted on my Twitter Page @TheSpacepony.

I dropped out of University to pursue a career in tech. I was studying a Bachelor of Information Technology majoring in Games and Graphics Programming at RMIT.

I made this game after being inspired by a similar riddle for the London Tube, which was widely credited for driving the internet to distraction. I ended up driving Melbourne to that same distraction.
